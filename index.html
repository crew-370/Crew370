A Bit About Us:

Venture Crew 370 has existed in various forms for over 20 years, evolving with the BSA program. Our focus on high adventure is pure and simple: we hike, backpack, sail, canoe, ski, climb, shoot, and start fires without matches. Through our adventures, we have learned how to lead and follow and have made connections for life. Such is the way of time spent in the wilderness!

Welcome to our adventure!
