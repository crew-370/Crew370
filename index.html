A bit about us.

VENTURE CREW 370 HAS BEEN AROUND IN VARIOUS FORMS FOR MORE THAN 20 YEARS, EVOLVING AS THE BSA PROGRAM HAS CHANGED OVER TIME. OUR FOCUS IS HIGH ADVENTURE PURE AND SIMPLE. WE HIKE. WE BACKPACK. WE SAIL. WE CANOE. WE SKI. WE CLIMB AND SHOOT AND START FIRES WITHOUT MATCHES. WE LEARN ABOUT HOW TO SURVIVE IN THE WILDERNESS - AND IN THE URBAN WILDERNESS AS WELL. THROUGH OUR ADVENTURES, WE LEARN HOW TO LEAD AND HOW TO FOLLOW, AND WE MAKE CONNECTIONS FOR LIFE. SUCH IS THE WAY OF TIME SPENT IN THE WILDERNESS! WELCOME TO OUR ADVENTURE!
